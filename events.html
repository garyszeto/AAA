Annual Events

Asiantation

An event organized by Asian Pacific American Coalition (APAC) that offers new incoming students an opportunities to get involved in Asian Culture.

Capture The Flag

Capture the Flag is a tournament hosted by the various APAC organizations to compete in a vigorous sports competition to strengthen bonds and invigorate team and organization spirit.

Family Olympiad

AAA Families are groups of AAA members to foster strong membership, and in family olympiad, they will compete in a series of relay races and challenging activities to promote bondings and foster strong relationships.

Semiformal

Semiformal is an AAA social event that takes place first semester to commemorate accomplishments and socialize with members to strengthen relationships.

Fashion Show

The annual modern and cultural dance performance that promotes AAA, Asians, and Asian American culture and identity that welcomes AAA Alumni to come and watch.

MAASU

A spring conference organized by the Midwest Asian American Students Union that unites people who are interested in Asian American culture in the Midwest area.

AAA Palooza

An event showcases a large variety of Asian American talent and culture from students both within and outside the APAC at UIUC.

Study Nights

Study Nights are frequent casual events hosted by AAA to create a synergistic study atmosphere for students to not only mutually work together but also to socialize and alleviate stress.

Taste Of Asia

Formal

The finale event in which members formally gather to reflect and commemorate the year and happily rejoice with the friendships and familial bondings that were made.
